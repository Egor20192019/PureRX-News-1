Has the solution to obesity been found or is it just another scam and scam? Laurie Greiner has invested $3.5 million in a weight loss product!

(ET, ) - It was the most watched episode in Shark Tank history when 'sharks' teamed up to buy 40% of the new dietary company for a staggering $6.5 Million.

Clinical herbalists, Ellie and Ann Davis, blew judges away with their scientific discovery that may decrease obesity over the world twicely. For seven years sisters have been working on formula that improves metabolism over the age of 40 and results in healthy body mass index (BMI). For the first time ever Lori Greiner invested $3.5 Million into Weight Loss product and provided full mentorship to entrepreneurs.

“We wanted to create safe, effective and life-changing weight loss product using the healing power of nature. We source only the cleanest ingredients, with a focus on organic and non-GMO”, confessed Ann on the show.

“The most we could hope for is some media exposure and maybe little funding,” said Ellie.

In the history of Shark Tank Ellie and Ann Davis are the only contestants that received investments from all five panel members.

After filming the episode sisters continue to work on a marketing and distribution strategy applying mentors advices into play.

"We hope to launch our product on the market soon, but in the meantime we are still working on mass production, logistics, packaging, warehousing and much more. With the help of our mentors and especially Laurie Greiner, I think we will hit the market in the next 9-11 months." said Ann.
